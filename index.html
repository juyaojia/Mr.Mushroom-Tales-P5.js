Welcome to Mrs.Mushroom Tales!


  • Moving left and right using 'A' and 'D' on keyboard, and jumping using 'W' key
  • Using 'G' for laser Gun, 'S' for sword, 'N' for cannon, and 'O' to fire or use the sword
  • Each bullet cost one coin
  • Kill Monsters or Collect coins scattered to get more coins
  • Jumping on Monsters to kill them immediately
  • You have three life, and you can get more by taking the health kit in the sky
  • Click 'PAUSE' to pause the game
  • Use the dropDown menu to choose the difficulty


How to win the game:



when the night has come, collect the magic mushroom using key 'C'!

(Remember that the mushroom is vulnerable. Don't hurt it with any weapon!)
